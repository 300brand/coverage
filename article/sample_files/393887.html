Source: OPM

In the spring of 2012, OPM asked 1.6 million Federal employees to provide their perspective on the business of government, and to tell us about their experience – what they see working, and what needs to be fixed. More than 687,000 answered the call, more than twice as many as any previous survey. These are the results.
